## Strategic Implications

### Must-haves to impress this panel:
1. **Voice AI via Bland** — 2/4 judges are from Bland. Using their API is almost mandatory
2. **AWS infrastructure** — Jon is AWS SA of the Year. Deploy on AWS (Bedrock, Lambda, etc.)
3. **Agent observability** — Abdul cares about this more than anything. Show traces, tool calls, decision paths
4. **Clean UX** — Spencer has a design background. The demo must look polished
5. **Real-world utility** — Jon's explicit judging criterion. Build something that solves a real problem
6. **Multi-step autonomous behavior** — The hackathon is literally called "Deep Agents". Show depth of reasoning

- **ArgusSquared** (2nd) — GitHub app reviewing PRs against regulations (CCPA, EU AI Act, BIPA)

### Pattern in winners:
- They solve a SPECIFIC, real business problem (not generic "chatbot" or "assistant")
- They demonstrate autonomous multi-step behavior (research → analyze → act)
- They integrate multiple tools/APIs in a meaningful pipeline
- They have a clear, memorable name and positioning
