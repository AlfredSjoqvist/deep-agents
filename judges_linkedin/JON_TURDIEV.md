250+ builders. 55 projects. AI agents are ready for real work.

This weekend at the MCP AI Agents Hackathon in San Francisco, teams turned ideas into working AI tools in just two days.

They built agents that do more than think.
They built agents that act.

🏆 Here are the top projects that stood out:

PitchScoop (Winner)
AI judge and coach for pitches. Scores live presentations, gives clear feedback, and tracks improvement over time.

Chicox
Transforms government procurement by auto-generating compliant RFPs in minutes using multi-agent orchestration and semantic matching.

autoRFP
End-to-end agent that finds RFPs, cleans and structures them, drafts citation-backed answers, self-evaluates coverage, and publishes ready-to-send proposals.

fridaySRE
AI SRE assistant built into your IDE. Diagnoses issues like “Why is my pod crashing?” with real-time Kubernetes and Prometheus monitoring and lightning-fast root cause analysis.

Hiveflow
Visual drag-and-drop canvas for building multi-agent systems. Connect models, tools, and APIs with live previews and full observability.

Unified Cloud
Generates clear, priced cloud architectures from a plain-language description and delivers ready-to-run Terraform files in minutes.

ReviewSense
Daily workflow that scrapes app reviews, clusters pain points, and generates prioritized, actionable responses for developers.
